How it works

Your daily dose of health in 3 simple steps

Step 1

Tell us what you like (and what not)

Never again waste time thinking about what to eat! Foodie will create a 100% personalized weekly meal plan just for you. It makes sure you get all the nutrients and vitamins you need, no matter what diet you follow.

Step 2

Approve your weekly meal plan

Once per week, approve the meal plan generated for you by Foodie. You can change ingredients, swap entire meals, or even add your own recipes or your special meals.

Step 3

Receive meals at convenient time

Best chefs in town will cook your selected meal every day, and we will deliver it to your door whenever works best for you. You can change delivery schedule and address daily.
